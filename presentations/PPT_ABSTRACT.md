## **Slide: Project Abstract**

### **EcoWorkConnect: An Integrated Digital Platform for Authentic Sustainability Service Management**

**EcoWorkConnect** is a specialized digital marketplace designed to bridge the gap between certified professionals and end-users in the fragmented sustainability sector. Built on a scalable MERN stack, the platform ensures verified selection of experts for services like Solar Panel Installation, Energy Audit, Rainwater Harvesting, Bio-Gas Services, Waste Management, Water Conservation, EV Charging Support, and Green Maintenance.

**Key Features:**
- Proximity-Based Filtering for hyper-local expert discovery
- Smart Credential Validation for professional certifications
- Real-time service tracking and status updates
- Secure 'Pay After Service' model to enhance user trust

**Technical Architecture:**
- Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- Backend: Node.js, Express.js with RESTful APIs
- Database: MongoDB with Mongoose ODM
- Security: JWT authentication, bcrypt.js, CORS

**Technical Achievements:**
- 15+ RESTful API endpoints
- 3 normalized database collections
- Complete authentication system with OTP verification
- Real-time booking functionality
- Mobile-responsive design

**Impact:**
- Supports SDG 7, 11, and 12
- Fosters clean energy adoption
- Builds sustainable urban communities
- Verified, location-aware service management

**Keywords:** MERN Stack, Sustainability Services, Digital Marketplace, Authentication Systems, Real-time Tracking
